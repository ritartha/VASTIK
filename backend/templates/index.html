About VASTIK

Bringing the rich heritage of Indian art and culture into the virtual worlds

VASTIK is a premium Second Life store dedicated to crafting authentic Indian-themed virtual goods. From intricately designed mesh structures inspired by ancient temples to vibrant textures reflecting the colors of Indian festivals, every product is created with passion and cultural respect. We bridge the gap between traditional artistry and the digital metaverse.

Mesh Creations

Hand-crafted 3D mesh products including temple gates, furniture, decorative items, and architectural elements with authentic Indian designs and optimized land impact.

LSL Scripts

Powerful and efficient LSL scripts for vendors, particle effects, interactive objects, and automation. Clean code with configuration notecards and documentation.

Textures

High-resolution texture packs featuring traditional Indian fabrics, henna patterns, rangoli designs, and ornamental motifs. Perfect for clothing and décor.
